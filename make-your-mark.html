Make Your Mark

In July 2020, our executive team of 4 young individuals made history by spearheading the inaugural Hwa Chong Global Digital Young Leaders Convention (HC-GDYLC) as the Executive Committee. After the groundbreaking convention, we were given the opportunity to present our educational pedagogy at the Global Learning Alliance to a group of educators hailing from prestigious educational institutions in Singapore, Finland and America. The affirmation of our novel and gamified method of cultivating essential soft skills gave us the confidence to take this beyond the school and venture into the community.

We soon embarked on a new journey and found ourselves competing in the Youth Action Challenge (YAC) Season 2 organised by NYC and MCCY. On 30 January 2021, we pitched our innovative idea of a gamified, collaborative platform for the cultivation of the essential soft skills to a group of government leaders and industry experts under the Jobs and The Future of Work category, where we offer youth with a never-seen-before opportunity to chart their own paths in the future workplace. We are honoured to have received the Promising Award.
