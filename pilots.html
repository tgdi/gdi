Our Pilots

We have held several physical and digital pilots thus far, engaging participants from various Junior Colleges and Polytechnics. They have tried their hands on our first industry experience: New Retail. The New Retail storyboard is imbued with more than 5 multifaceted challenges and tasks for them to resolve for the welfare of their very own store.

Through our post-pilot surveys, over 80% of our participants found the experience enjoyable and felt that they got a better understanding of the new industry. Many also felt that they developed their soft skills and are more prepared for the future workplace after completing the industry experience.

While we develop our storyboard further, we are actively holding new pilots to enhance our industry experiences to provide the best experience for you.
