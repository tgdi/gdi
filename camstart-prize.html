International CAMSTART Accelerator 3rd prize

Your Social Enterprise Journey Awaits.

Spearheaded by a team of students hailing from the University of Cambridge, Camstart Accelerator is a tailor-made youth social entrepreneurship programme for students to build and develop their social impact projects. Supported by a global community of youth changemakers, social entrepreneurs and the Cambridge team, the Camstart Accelerator programme culminates with a pitch competition.

The Global Digital Initiative team pitched their project to a distinguished panel of judges and was awarded the third place globally.
