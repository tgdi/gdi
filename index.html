About GDI 2021

TGDI is a budding educational consultancy which aims to develop virtual collaborative and gamified experiences for youth to better prepare themselves for the future workspace. Through the unique pedagogy of gamification, where game-design elements are applied, our platform focuses on the development of essential soft skills in demand.

The emerging contours of the new world of work are becoming a lived reality for employees and businesses on an international scale, with prolonged recession, high levels of structural unemployment and global disruptions topping the list of worries for risk professionals globally. Nevertheless, there remains inherent opportunities for our youth to flourish in this volatile world, with soft skills, such as bold leadership and adaptive teamwork, critical and innovative thinking and complex problem solving, becoming crucial determiners. Hence, our platform draws from principles of gamification as a novel pedagogy to create a fun yet impactful experience for our target audience.

Our Logo

The logo of the of The Global Digital Initiative (TGDI) explores the concept of identity in our VUCA world. GDYLC takes on a bold yet ever-relevant ideology of using technology to dimensionally reimagine the way we connect with one another, a vision that is represented succinctly using the motif of the thumbprint in the logo.
