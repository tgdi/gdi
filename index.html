An Alternate Reality

Find yourself immersed in a “role” in a simulated crisis in the workplace, a paradigm shift in the future economy or a corporate competition!

You will experience different industries set in the future workplace in a choose-your-own-story format.

  • Learn more

The Future Workspace

Have fun venturing into challenges in an immersive landscape woven by your coordinators, make important decisions to chart your own paths in the future workspace and respond to new developments in the alternate reality, with your ultimate goal being to adapt to constant change in the best way possible. These challenges are closely related to promoting the identification, exercise and development of crucial soft skills and decision-making strategies.

  • Learn more

Are You Ready?

You will work together with your fellow participants to raise a set of game indicators that determine the success metric of your team and are thus also challenged to make strategic decisions that will benefit your team and maximise your points.
